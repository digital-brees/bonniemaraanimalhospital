Pet insurance

How insurance works.

Pet insurance works a little differently than human health insurance. You pay for your pet’s care at the time of the visit, then submit your invoice to your provider for reimbursement. We’re happy to give you the detailed, itemized paperwork you need to file a claim.

  1. 1

    Pay for your visit at the time of service.

  2. 2

    We provide a detailed, itemized invoice.

  3. 3

    Submit it to your insurer for reimbursement.
