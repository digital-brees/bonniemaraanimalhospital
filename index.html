Bonnie Mara, in two languages.

I chose the name “Bonnie Mara” to reflect both my Scottish roots and the natural beauty of our surroundings here in Santa Barbara. “Bonnie” is what my granny called everything beautiful — the landscape, our family, a quiet morning.

“Mara” means sea, in Gaelic. It captures my deep connection to Santa Barbara, the beaches I’ve walked with my dogs, and the calm that lives in the water. The ocean embodies peace and interconnectedness of all living beings — values that deeply resonate with our mission.

Dr. Laura Putnam

The practice

Comprehensive care, quietly done.

For dogs and cats — from first puppyhood and kittenhood through the senior years.

  1. 01

    Wellness

    Annual and semi‑annual visits tailored to age, breed, and species. Good habits, vaccines, nutrition — in your time, on your pet’s pace.

  2. 02

    Surgery

    Routine spays and neuters; complex soft tissue; emergency cases including GDV, splenectomy, bladder, pyometra, foxtails, and trauma surgery.

  3. 03

    Dentistry & COHAT

    Complete oral health assessment and treatment under general anesthesia. Every procedure includes dental radiographs and a thorough oral exam.

  4. 04

    Diagnostics & imaging

    Full in‑house lab plus Idexx reference. ImageVue DR50 Plus digital x‑ray. Abdominal and emergency ultrasound.

  5. 05

    Urgent & same‑day

    Vet visits are often unexpected. We’ll do our best to accommodate you in your time of need — without sending you somewhere unfamiliar.

  6. 06

    End‑of‑life

    Hospice, palliative, and euthanasia services with dignity. On your pet’s terms, in a space that feels right for your family.
