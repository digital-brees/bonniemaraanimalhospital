## Why work for Bonnie Mara Animal Hospital?

At Bonnie Mara, we know how important each member of the veterinary team is and successful patient care requires a cohesive, collaborative unit. Veterinary medicine can be hard work, but working for a small, independent company that cares about each individual employee and values their input and experience makes coming to work each day worth it! But, most importantly, we know work isn't your whole life and offer flexible scheduling to make sure you aren't missing the important moments in life too!

## Are you hiring?

Yes! We are currently hiring for client service representatives, registered veterinary technicians (RVTs) and vet assistants. Please apply here {INDEED_URL}.

## Will team members be cross trained?

Yes, we are starting with a small team of 5-6 in a big hospital! All hands on deck from the beginning! Anyone who has worked with Dr Putnam knows she isn't above answering the phone or cleaning a cage. We will be a true team! Don't worry, we finished the painting, but the start up team will be doing everything from helping establish medical protocols to hanging up the art work! Or learning how to use the new air-fryer microwave! It is going to be fun!

## What benefits do you offer?

Besides coming to work with an awesome team every day and saving lives? We offer competitive wages, health insurance, dental insurance, vision plan, retirement plan, pet care benefits, paid time off and of course all nights, weekends and holidays off. Veterinary professionals will also have a continuing education budget.
