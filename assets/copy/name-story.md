I chose the name "Bonnie Mara" for the hospital to reflect both my Scottish roots as well as the natural beauty of our surroundings here in Santa Barbara.

The word "Bonnie" holds a special place in my heart, capturing the charm and beauty of my childhood in Scotland while bringing to mind my granny, who used it affectionately to describe everything from the breathtaking landscape to her beloved family.

"Mara," meaning "sea/ocean" in Gaelic, captures my deep connection to Santa Barbara, a place that has shaped my life since my early years. The beautiful beaches evoke memories of leisurely walks with my dogs, joyful reunions with friends, and active family days spent swimming and playing. The ocean embodies peace and interconnectedness of all living beings — values that deeply resonate with our mission.

By naming the hospital "Bonnie Mara," I aim to blend these influences, creating a space that reflects the calming essence of nature. Our commitment is to provide low-stress, high quality care for pets and their owners while fostering trust and collaboration. At Bonnie Mara Animal Hospital, we strive to make each visit a positive experience for both our clients and their beloved companions.
