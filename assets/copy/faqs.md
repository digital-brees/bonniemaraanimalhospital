## Practical Questions

### Will you offer after hours emergency care or overnight boarding?
Unfortunately no, we are not zoned to be open for 24 hours. Any patients requiring overnight care will be referred to a 24-hour facility.

### Will you offer pet grooming?
No, we are focusing on all aspects of veterinary care and will leave grooming to those who do it professionally.

### What will your hours be?
Monday through Friday 8am–6pm. Appointments preferred.

### Will you provide urgent or same-day appointments?
Yes, we know often visits to the vet are unexpected and will do our best to accommodate you during your time of need.

### Is parking available?
Yes! We have plenty of parking.

### Will you perform surgery?
Yes, in addition to routine surgery (spays/neuters/laceration repairs/mass removals), Dr Putnam also is comfortable and competent in more complex soft tissue surgeries as well as emergency surgeries (including gastro-intestinal foreign bodies, gastropexy, GDV surgery, splenectomy, cesarean sections, bladder surgery, pyometra surgery, foxtail removals, abscess surgeries and trauma surgery).

### Will you offer dental care?
Yes, Bonnie Mara Animal Hospital will offer COHAT procedures (complete oral health assessment and treatment) under general anesthesia. We are passionate about dentistry and keeping our patients' mouths healthy. Every dental procedure includes dental radiographs (x-rays) and a complete oral exam in addition to cleaning and polishing the teeth. Dr Putnam is also comfortable with all types of extractions as well as more advanced dental procedures (root planing/bonded sealants/guided tissue regeneration/oronasal fistula repair) when applicable.

### Will you offer lab tests?
Yes, we have a full in-house lab (including blood tests, urine tests, cytology, snap tests) and have a relationship with Idexx commercial veterinary laboratory to offer more comprehensive testing.

### Will you have digital x-ray?
Yes, we have the ImageVue DR50 Plus digital x-ray system which provides superior x-ray images with the lowest dose of radiation for our patients. These images can also be digitally sent for review by board certified veterinary radiologists.

### Will you offer ultrasound?
Yes, Bonnie Mara Animal Hospital will perform abdominal ultrasounds as well as emergency ultrasounds.
