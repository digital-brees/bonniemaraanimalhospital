Santa Barbara, CA Opening Summer 2026

A letter from Dr. Putnam

A new kind of veterinary care is coming to Santa Barbara.

For dogs and cats — and the people who love them.

Independently owned. Low‑stress by design. Opening this summer at 4860 Calle Real, directly across from In‑N‑Out off the 101.

We’ll email you once when appointments open. No marketing, no list-sharing.

· The name ·

Bonnie Mara, in two languages.

“Bonnie” is what my granny called everything beautiful in Scotland — the landscape, our family, a quiet morning.

“Mara” means sea, in Gaelic. It’s how I’ve always felt about Santa Barbara — the place that shaped me, the beaches I walked with my dogs, the calm that lives in the water.

Together, they describe the kind of hospital I want to build: calm, beautiful, rooted in something older than the building itself.

— Dr. Le Putnam

What we’ll offer

Wellness, surgery, dentistry, diagnostics, urgent care — all under one roof, for dogs and cats.

Independently owned by Dr. Le Putnam, so we have the time to focus on each visit, each pet, and each family. Not a one‑size‑fits‑all approach.
