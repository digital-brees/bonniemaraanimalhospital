Why work for Bonnie Mara Animal Hospital?

At Bonnie Mara, we know how important each member of the veterinary team is — successful patient care requires a cohesive, collaborative unit.

Anyone who has worked with Dr. Putnam knows she isn’t above answering the phone or cleaning a cage. We will be a true team — all hands on deck from the beginning.

Veterinary medicine can be hard work, but working for a small, independent company that cares about each individual employee and values their input and experience makes coming to work each day worth it.

Most importantly, we know work isn’t your whole life. We offer flexible scheduling so you aren’t missing the important moments in life too.

Besides coming to work with an awesome team every day, and saving lives?

We offer competitive wages, health insurance, dental insurance, vision plan, retirement plan, pet care benefits, paid time off, and a continuing education budget — plus all nights, weekends, and holidays off.
