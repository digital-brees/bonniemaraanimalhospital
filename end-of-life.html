Compassionate Care

End-of-life care.

Here with you

Our compassionate team is made up of loving pet owners, so we understand that saying goodbye is one of the most challenging experiences you will face. We’ll be here for you throughout the entire process.

Comfort & quality of life

Hospice & palliative care.

Hospice and palliative care focus on providing comfort and preserving quality of life for pets with terminal illnesses. Each pet’s journey is unique, so our team works closely with you to create a personalized care plan that addresses pain, mobility, nutrition, and any other needs. It is appropriate for any pet with an incurable illness when treatments to prolong life are unlikely to improve its quality.

A peaceful passing

Euthanasia.

Choosing euthanasia can be a heart-wrenching decision, but it is often the kindest option when a pet’s quality of life has significantly declined. Our experienced veterinarians can assist with quality-of-life assessments, euthanasia planning, and emotional support, helping you feel confident in your decisions. Our goal is to provide peace and comfort for you and your pet, while personalizing the experience to meet your needs.

In a familiar place

In-home euthanasia.

In-home euthanasia allows your pet to pass in a familiar, peaceful setting, surrounded by their favorite people. We work with you to ensure your wishes for your pet’s final moments are fulfilled—providing a stress-free experience, respecting your privacy, and offering the emotional support and guidance you need.

Here for you

For your pet, and your family.

From assessing quality of life to planning a peaceful passing, we will support you and your family every step of the way. When you’re ready to talk, we’re here.
